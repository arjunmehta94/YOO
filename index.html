Feather

Make your pen smarter.

A simple and versatile grip which plugs into your pen to record your writing.

Bridging the gap

Feather offers to bridge the gap between the physical and the software world. Writing or drawing on paper is a very organic and expressive feeling which is very familiar to us.

But in the world of computing, there is very little place for physical objects such as paper. What is important is the idea.

Feather helps preserve that idea expressed on the paper in software.

Versatility

Attach it to a Pen, Pencil, Marker, your finger or even a Chopstick if you want! The design of Feather is to clip and play! Moreover, the design and ergonomics make writing a pleasure.

Expanding the Horizon

Feather makes the pen more than what it is. Software support such as Handwriting Recognition, Peer-to-Peer teaching platforms, online form-filling, biometric applications etc. can enhance the use of this simple tool by miles.
